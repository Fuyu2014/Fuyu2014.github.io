Travel & Company Visit in Suzhou, China

December 8 - December 22, 2014

In this winter, I joined NUSRI Winter program. It is a fascinating program that provide me a chance to explore China "Wu" culture, Chinese Architecture and Chinese ways of doing business. It also deepened my understanding of China, particularly with respect to its booming economy. I visited Suzhou-Singapore Industries Park, a joint venture between Singapore and China that is home to a wide variety of industries.
I also made many friends from NUS, SMU and The University of Western Australia. I am so excited about that, hahaha :-D
